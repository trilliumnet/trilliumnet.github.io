Resources for Teachers and Organizers

Geography Fairs are a great way to get students learning geography. But planning and organizing a fair can be a great challenge. Here, you will find some resources to help plan a successful school geography fair.

  • How to Organize
    		• This page provides assistance on how to organize and host a Geography Fair.
  • Sample Guidelines
    		• These guidelines can be used or adapted to fit a fair at your school.
  • Letter to parents
    		• The involvement of parents is an important part of a geography fair. This sample letter can be adapted and sent to parents to make them aware of the geography fair.
